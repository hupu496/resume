About this project

A full-featured OTT (Over-The-Top) streaming platform similar to Netflix, developed using Laravel. The application allows users to stream movies and web series on demand, manage subscriptions, track watch history, and receive personalized content recommendations. The platform supports secure video streaming, role-based administration, and scalable content delivery.
